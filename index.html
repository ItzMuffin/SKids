Our goal is to make outdoor activities safer for both the children and the teachers.

Outdoor activities are essential in the school life of children. We believe that with SKids we can make them safer and thus more fun, by providing the teachers with a tool which can help them in their supervising duties. In this way, the teachers will spend less time worring and more time building their relationship with the children.

Learn more

What is it

SKids is a service for preschool which helps increase the safety of the children during the time they spend outside the building.

How it works

SKids integrates hardware and software. A cloud based service connects the SKids web portal to the SKids app, which, together with the SKids beacons, helps the teachers determine the location of the children, and get a notification when they go too far away.

The web interface

Through the web interface you will be able to create and manage new outdoor activities, assign teachers to different activities and have an overview on which children are taking part in which activity.

The app

With the app, the teachers will be able to check which activity they were assigned to, connect to their beacon and start the monitoring process. The teachers can use the app to have an overview on where and how far away the children are and set the maximum distance they are allowed to go. The app also warns the teacher with a notification when a child gets too far away

The beacons

The beacons are the heart of SKids. They use Bluetooth LE signals to determine the distance between the child's beacon and the teacher's beacon, which, together with the smartphone GPS, give a great degree of accuracy.
